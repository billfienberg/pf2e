# Encounter with Kundal the Werewolf

## Summary

The party faces a deadly lycanthrope threat in the form of Kundal, a fierce werewolf. The battle proves challenging as the werewolf's supernatural resilience and savage attacks push the heroes to their limits. Crio is knocked unconscious twice during the intense combat, but the party ultimately prevails. They recover a finely crafted longbow as their reward. This encounter demonstrates the increasing dangers they face as rulers of the expanding kingdom.
