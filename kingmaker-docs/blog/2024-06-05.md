# Kingdom Building Begins

## Summary

The party officially begins their reign as the rulers of a new kingdom. They establish an Oligarchy government focused on Industry and Engineering, gaining the Insider Trading kingdom feat. The newly crowned leaders spend their first Resource Points on commodities and hire adventurers to explore new hexes. This marks the beginning of the kingdom building phase of their campaign, transitioning from adventuring heroes to political rulers managing resources and expansion.
