# Old Sycamore Depths - Gremlin Extermination

## Summary

The party ventured deeper into the Old Sycamore tree, engaging in multiple combat encounters against gremlins, mitflits, and various creatures. They fought their way through several rooms, rescuing and working alongside Skogir the kobold. Key encounters included battles against Grabbles and other named gremlins, as well as a fight with Quoggy and centipedes. The session involved tactical combat with environmental challenges like climbing and positioning. The party discovered a map of kobolds and prepared to take Skogir home before continuing their search for missing NPCs.
