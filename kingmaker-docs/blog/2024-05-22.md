# Victory Over the Stag Lord

## Summary

The climactic battle at the Stag Lord's fort continues. The party defeats Dovan and faces the Stag Lord himself in an epic confrontation. Key allies emerge including Akiros Ismort, who helps open the gates. After a grueling fight involving an owlbear, the party finally defeats the notorious Stag Lord, marking a major milestone in their campaign. The owlbear remains alive but trapped, setting up future challenges.
