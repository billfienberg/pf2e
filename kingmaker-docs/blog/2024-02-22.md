## Summary
The party explores the hexes around their territory, discovering an old mine, a magical cache with valuable items including a wand and spellbook, and encounters four kobolds at a moonradish patch. They capture one kobold, name him Jerry, and learn about troubles at the Old Sycamore. They also hear rumors about a missing trapper named Breeg Olevanch who may have joined bandits near the Thorn River.
